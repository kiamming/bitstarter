Something significant happened in your life, and you want to take a picture, record an audio of the moment, take a video, or simply pen some thoughts down. But you don't want to share it with the whole world by default. Instead, you want it to be your online diary (i.e. private by default), and you choose what you want to share, and who you want to share it with.

"Jeuny" is pronounced "journey", and it stands for your personal life's journey. The first part of the word, "jeu", is French for "game", and it means that your life's journey is like a game. Not that your life's journey isn't serious, but in the sense that you have different missions to achieve in your life, and Jeuny.com is there to record down all the milestones that you encounter in your life.

About Jeuny.com...

"How I wish I have a multimedia personal diary..." Those are the first words that came to my mind when I was taking photos and videos of my firstborn's milestones. My wife would print out certain photos and organize them into a scrapbook, but for lazy me, an online diary was what I was hungering for.

FAQ

What is the key difference between Jeuny.com and other social media apps?
Most social media apps are meant for sharing i.e. public (at least with friends) by default, but Jeuny.com is meant to be an intimate place for personal reflection, so it is private by default.
Why should I use Jeuny.com?
If you want to have a place to capture significant moments of your life, a place where you can pen down intimate thoughts and feelings, in short, an online diary that is directly linked to your mobile device (your main instrument to capture these significant moments), then you should use Jeuny.com.
